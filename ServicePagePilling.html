Spotify

This is my preferred music streaming service, Spotify gives me the ability to have a never ending music library ready at the push of a button. Through cloud storage I have access to these services anytime and anywhere. As a college student this is an invaluable service for me and has become part of my everyday use.

Amazon Prime Video

Amazon Prime provides an incredible on demand movie and T.V. streaming service. You have the ability to access high quality entertainment whenever you want due to the use of Amazon Web Services(AWS).

Google Drive

In terms of usability, Google Drive is fantastic for group projects and the general storage of documents for college, this can be credited to the 15gb of free cloud storage. It gives me the ability to have access to project work whenever I need and most importantly allows me to update files for anybody in a group to have access to. This ease of use has been key for me in successful group work.
